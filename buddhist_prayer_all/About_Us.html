About Us We are a non-profit & non-denomination initiative to bring Buddhism online, making it accessible to people of various Buddhist traditions around the world. Our work relies on dedicated volunteer, and advisor working remotely and online in various countries. Together we bring forth our goal of making Buddhism more accessible and engage communities for the benefit of all beings.
